SRC  Incharge

STUDENTS' REPRESENTATIVE COUNCIL (JIT SRC) is a student body of the college consisting of student representatives from all the branches. The function of the student council is based upon parliamentary procedures. Ideas are presented, voted upon and confirmed by the students' body. Any student interested in leadership, organizational behavior, event planning or becoming more involved in the college are welcome to become involved.

The SRC is mainly involved in conducting and organizing intra and inter-college activities.SRC's work democratically to represent the student body in Institute's decision-making and organise ways for students to participate in and enjoy college life. All these activities throughout the year help the students to improve on their communication skills, presentation skills, and organizational skills and help boosting the self confidence of the students and help them explore their latent talents.

We wish the Team very best.
